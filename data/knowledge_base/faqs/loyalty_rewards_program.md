# Loyalty Rewards Program
Source: faqs/loyalty_rewards_program.md

**Last Updated:** January 2025
**Applies to:** All orders on ShopEase.com and the ShopEase mobile app

## Earning Points
With the ShopEase Rewards program, you earn **1 point for every Rs. 10 spent** on eligible purchases. 
- Points are credited to your account after the 30-day standard return window closes.
- Points are valid for **12 months** from the date they are earned.

**Exceptions:** Points are NOT earned on the purchase of Gift Cards, the ShopEase Plus subscription fee, or on cancelled/returned orders.

## Redeeming Points
You can check your points balance by visiting **My Account → Rewards Dashboard**.
- **100 points = Rs. 10 off** your next order.
- You must have a minimum of 500 points to redeem them at checkout.

## ShopEase Plus Membership
For our most frequent shoppers, we offer **ShopEase Plus**.
- **Cost:** Rs. 499/year.
- **Benefits:** Free standard shipping (5–7 business days) on all orders, regardless of order value (normally requires spending above Rs. 499).
