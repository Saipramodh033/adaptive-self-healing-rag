# Gift Cards and Vouchers
Source: faqs/gift_cards_and_vouchers.md

**Last Updated:** January 2025
**Applies to:** All orders on ShopEase.com and the ShopEase mobile app

## Purchasing and Redemption
- **Purchasing:** You can purchase digital Gift Cards ranging from Rs. 500 to Rs. 10,000. They are delivered instantly via email.
- **Redemption:** To redeem a Gift Card, enter the 16-digit code at checkout in the "Gift Card or Promo Code" field. The balance will be applied to your order total.

## Balance Check and Expiry
- **Checking Balance:** Visit **My Account** → **Gift Cards** to check your current balance.
- **Expiry:** All ShopEase Gift Cards expire **2 years (24 months)** from the date of issue. Once expired, the balance cannot be reinstated.

## Restrictions
- **Non-Refundable:** Gift cards are non-refundable and cannot be exchanged for cash.
- **Lost or Stolen:** We are not responsible for lost or stolen gift card codes. They cannot be replaced.
- **Combinations:** You may use a Gift Card to pay for an order, but Gift Cards cannot be used to purchase other Gift Cards. Furthermore, you cannot apply promotional discount codes to the purchase of a Gift Card itself.
