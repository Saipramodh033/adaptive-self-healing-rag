# Promotions and Coupons
Source: faqs/promotions_and_coupons.md

**Last Updated:** January 2025
**Applies to:** All orders on ShopEase.com and the ShopEase mobile app

## Finding Promo Codes
Active promotional codes can be found:
- On the homepage banner.
- On the product details page.
- In promotional emails sent to subscribed users.

## Applying Codes
To apply a promo code, enter it in the "Promo Code" box during checkout and click **Apply**.
- **One Code Per Order:** You can only use one promotional code per order. Codes cannot be stacked.
- **Sale Items:** Promo codes cannot be applied to items that are already discounted during major sale events (e.g., End of Season Sale).
- **Expired Codes:** Expired codes cannot be honored under any circumstances.

## Sale Events
ShopEase runs several major sale events throughout the year, such as the "Big Billion Days" and "End of Season Sale". During these events:
- Standard shipping (5–7 business days) may experience slight delays due to high volume.
- ShopEase Plus members get 24-hour early access to sale pricing.
