## Placing Orders

### What payment methods does ShopEase accept?

ShopEase accepts the following payment methods:

- **Credit cards:** Visa, Mastercard, American Express, RuPay
- **Debit cards:** All major Indian bank debit cards
- **UPI:** Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM, Amazon Pay
- **Net banking:** All major Indian banks
- **ShopEase Wallet:** Prepaid wallet with instant checkout
- **EMI:** Available on orders above Rs. 3,000 (select banks; 3, 6, 9, 12 month plans)
- **Buy Now Pay Later:** ShopEase PayLater (eligibility based on credit check)
- **Cash on Delivery (COD):** Available on orders up to Rs. 10,000 in eligible pin codes
- **Gift cards:** ShopEase digital gift cards (Rs. 100 to Rs. 10,000 denominations)
- **PayPal:** Available for international customers

---

### Can I modify my order after placing it?

Order modifications are possible **only within 15 minutes** of placing the order, and only if it has not yet been confirmed by the seller or dispatched.

To modify an order:
1. Go to **My Orders**
2. Click on the order you want to modify
3. Select **Modify Order** (visible only within the 15-minute window)

Modifications allowed: delivery address, quantity (if stock permits), payment method.

Modifications NOT allowed after confirmation: product selection, seller, or shipping speed downgrade.

If the modification window has passed, cancel the order and place a new one.

---

### How do I cancel my order?

You can cancel an order before it is shipped:

1. Go to **My Orders**
2. Click on the order
3. Select **Cancel Order**
4. Choose a cancellation reason
5. Confirm cancellation

**After shipping:** Orders in transit cannot be cancelled. Wait for delivery and then initiate a return.

**Refund for cancelled orders:**
- Prepaid orders: Refund initiated within 24 hours, credited to the original payment method in 5–7 business days
- COD orders: No refund needed (payment not collected)

---

### How can I track my order?

Track your order in three ways:

1. **ShopEase App or Website:** My Orders → click on the order → Track Package
2. **SMS updates:** Sent automatically to your registered mobile number at each milestone
3. **Courier website:** Use the tracking number from your shipping confirmation email on the courier's website

Tracking usually activates **4–6 hours** after the order is picked up by the courier.

---

### What is the order confirmation process?

After placing your order:
1. You receive an **order confirmation email** immediately (with order number)
2. The seller confirms availability within 1–2 hours (you receive a seller confirmation email)
3. When the order is dispatched, you receive a **shipping confirmation** with tracking details

If you do not receive an order confirmation email within 15 minutes, check your spam folder. If not found, contact support with your registered email address.

## Payments and Billing

### How do I request an invoice?

A GST invoice is automatically generated for all orders and is available in:
- **My Orders** → select the order → **Download Invoice**
- The invoice is also attached to your order confirmation email

For business purchases requiring a specific GST number on the invoice, add your GST number in **Account Settings** → **Business Details** before placing the order.

---

### How do gift cards work?

ShopEase gift cards:
- Are available in denominations of Rs. 100, Rs. 250, Rs. 500, Rs. 1,000, Rs. 2,000, Rs. 5,000, Rs. 10,000
- Can be purchased on shopease.com/gift-cards
- Are delivered via email within 24 hours of purchase
- Never expire
- Can be used for any purchase on ShopEase
- Cannot be used to purchase other gift cards
- Cannot be exchanged for cash
- Can be checked via shopease.com/check-gift-card

To redeem: Enter the gift card code at checkout in the **Gift Card / Promo Code** field.

---

### How do promo codes and discount coupons work?

- Enter your promo code in the **Promo Code** field at checkout, before payment
- Promo codes cannot be applied after an order is placed
- Only one promo code can be used per order (unless stated otherwise)
- Promo codes cannot be combined with other ongoing sale discounts unless explicitly stated
- Promo codes have validity dates — check the expiry before use
- If a code is not working, verify: correct capitalization, order eligibility, minimum cart value, product category exclusions

---

### What is the EMI payment process?

EMI is available on orders above Rs. 3,000 for eligible card holders:

1. Add items to cart and proceed to checkout
2. Select **Credit Card** or **Debit Card** as payment method
3. Click **Convert to EMI** after entering card details
4. Select your preferred EMI tenure (3, 6, 9, or 12 months)
5. Review the EMI amount and any applicable processing fee
6. Confirm payment

**Note:** EMI availability depends on your bank and card type. No-cost EMI is available on select products and banks — look for the "No-Cost EMI" tag on product pages.

---

### My payment was deducted but the order was not placed. What should I do?

This can happen when there is a brief disconnection between the payment gateway and ShopEase. In most cases, the amount is automatically refunded within 5–7 business days.

To investigate:
1. Check **My Orders** — if the order is not visible, the payment was not captured
2. Check your bank statement — if the amount was debited, it will be refunded automatically
3. If not refunded within 7 business days, contact support with your bank transaction reference number
