# Contact and Support Channels
Source: faqs/contact_and_support_channels.md

**Last Updated:** January 2025
**Applies to:** All orders on ShopEase.com and the ShopEase mobile app

## Support Directory
Here is a complete directory of all ShopEase contact channels. Please use the channel most relevant to your issue to ensure the fastest response.

### Email Channels
- **support@shopease.com** — General inquiries, account issues, missing loyalty points.
- **returns@shopease.com** — Stalled refunds, defective item reports, return scheduling.
- **shipping@shopease.com** — Lost packages, delayed delivery, international shipping.
- **security@shopease.com** — Account lockouts, unauthorized access, data deletion requests.
- **business@shopease.com** — Bulk orders (10+ units), B2B invoicing, business accounts.

### Phone Support
- **1-800-SHOP-EASE**
- Available: Monday–Friday, 9 AM–6 PM IST.
- Best for: Urgent inquiries that cannot wait for an email response.

### Live Chat
- **shopease.com live chat**
- Available: Monday–Saturday, 9 AM–8 PM IST.
- Best for: Quick questions, navigating the website, basic troubleshooting.

### App Self-Service
- **My Orders → Report a Problem**
- Best for: The fastest way to report a missing grocery item, request a cancellation, or initiate a return.
