# Reviews and Ratings
Source: faqs/reviews_and_ratings.md

**Last Updated:** January 2025
**Applies to:** All orders on ShopEase.com and the ShopEase mobile app

## Leaving a Review
You can leave a review and rating for any product you have purchased.
- **Eligibility:** You must have successfully purchased and received the item. You cannot review items you haven't bought.
- **How to review:** Go to **My Orders**, select the delivered item, and click **Write a Review**.

## Review Moderation
To maintain a high-quality platform, all reviews go through moderation before being published.
- The moderation timeline is typically **48 hours**.
- Reviews containing profanity, personally identifiable information, or irrelevant content (e.g., reviewing the shipping carrier instead of the product) will be rejected.

## Reporting Fake Reviews
If you suspect a review is fake or violates our guidelines, click the "Report" flag next to the review. Our moderation team will investigate.

## Seller Responses
Third-party sellers have the ability to respond publicly to reviews left on their products.

## Contact Information
If your legitimate review was rejected incorrectly:
- Email: support@shopease.com
- Live Chat: shopease.com (Monday–Saturday, 9 AM–8 PM IST)
