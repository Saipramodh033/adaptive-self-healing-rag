## Product Details and Availability

### How do I find the right size?

ShopEase provides detailed size guides on product pages for clothing, footwear, and accessories.

**To find a size guide:**
1. Open the product page
2. Click **Size Guide** (available next to the size selector for eligible categories)
3. Measure yourself as instructed and compare to the chart

**General sizing tips:**
- ShopEase displays both Indian and international size standards (S/M/L/XL and numeric sizes)
- For footwear, measure your foot length in cm and match to the size chart
- For kids' clothing, size is usually based on age and height — check the guide for exact measurements
- If you are between sizes, we generally recommend sizing up for comfort

If the size guide does not help, check the product's customer reviews — buyers often mention whether an item runs small, large, or true to size.

### How do I check if a product is available in my area?

Product availability depends on your delivery pin code:

1. Open the product page
2. Find the **Check Availability** box (usually near the Add to Cart button)
3. Enter your pin code
4. The page will show estimated delivery dates and whether the product ships to your location

If a product does not deliver to your area, it is usually due to:
- Logistics partner restrictions in your region
- Product category restrictions (e.g., lithium batteries, oversized items)
- Seller not shipping to your pin code

### What does "Pre-Order" mean?

A pre-order allows you to purchase a product before it is officially released or back in stock.

**How pre-orders work:**
- Your payment is collected at the time of ordering
- The product ships on or before the listed release date
- You will receive shipping confirmation when the product is dispatched
- If ShopEase cannot fulfil your pre-order, a full refund is automatically issued

**Cancelling a pre-order:** Pre-orders can be cancelled anytime before the product ships via **My Orders** → **Cancel Order**. Full refund within 5–7 business days.

### How do I check product availability notifications?

If a product is out of stock:

1. Open the product page
2. Click **Notify Me When Available**
3. Make sure your email and mobile notifications are enabled in Account Settings

You will receive an email and/or push notification when the product is back in stock. Notifications do not reserve the item — add it to cart quickly as stock may be limited.

## Product Reviews and Comparisons

### How do product reviews work?

Reviews on ShopEase are from verified buyers only — you can only write a review for a product you have purchased and received.

**To write a review:**
1. Go to **My Orders** → find the delivered order
2. Click **Write a Review**
3. Rate the product (1–5 stars)
4. Write your review (minimum 20 characters)
5. Upload photos or videos (optional, up to 5 images or 1 video)
6. Submit

Reviews are moderated and published within 48 hours. ShopEase removes reviews that:
- Contain abusive or offensive language
- Are not about the product (e.g., complaints about shipping — use support instead)
- Are suspected to be fake or incentivised

---

### How do I compare products?

To compare up to 4 products side-by-side:

1. In search results or category pages, click the **Compare** checkbox on each product card (up to 4 products)
2. Click **Compare Now** in the floating bar at the bottom of the screen
3. The comparison table shows specifications, price, ratings, and key features side-by-side

The compare feature is available for electronics, appliances, and other specification-heavy categories.

---

### How do I know if a product listing is accurate?

ShopEase requires sellers to provide accurate product descriptions, images, and specifications. However, for third-party seller listings:

- **Verified by ShopEase:** Listings marked with this badge have been quality-checked by ShopEase
- **Customer reviews:** Read reviews for real-world feedback on accuracy
- **Questions and Answers:** Check the Q&A section on the product page — other customers or the seller may have answered your specific question

If you receive a product that significantly differs from its description (wrong colour, missing features, etc.), you are eligible for a return under our **Item does not match description** return reason — even if the standard return window has passed.
