## Account Access

### How do I reset my password?

If you have forgotten your password:

1. Go to shopease.com and click **Sign In**
2. Click **Forgot Password?** below the sign-in form
3. Enter the email address associated with your ShopEase account
4. Click **Send Reset Link**
5. Check your email for a password reset link (valid for 15 minutes)
6. Click the link and enter your new password
7. Your password must be at least 8 characters and contain a mix of letters and numbers

If you do not receive the email within 5 minutes:
- Check your spam or junk folder
- Verify you are using the correct email address
- Try the process again or contact support

---

### How do I change my email address?

To change your registered email address:

1. Go to **Account Settings** → **Personal Information** → **Edit Email**
2. Enter your current password to verify your identity
3. Enter the new email address
4. Click **Send Verification**
5. Check the new email address for a verification link and click it
6. Your email address will be updated immediately

**Note:** You will be signed out of all devices after changing your email. Sign back in with your new email and current password.

---

### How do I enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)?

Two-Factor Authentication adds a second layer of security to your account:

1. Go to **Account Settings** → **Security & Privacy** → **Two-Factor Authentication**
2. Select your preferred 2FA method:
   - **OTP via SMS:** A one-time code sent to your mobile number
   - **OTP via Email:** A one-time code sent to your email
   - **Authenticator App:** Google Authenticator, Authy, or Microsoft Authenticator
3. Follow the setup instructions for your chosen method
4. Save your **backup codes** in a secure location (used if you lose access to your 2FA method)

Once enabled, you will be asked for a 2FA code every time you sign in from a new device.

---

### I am locked out of my account. What should I do?

Your account may be temporarily locked after multiple failed sign-in attempts (5 attempts trigger a 30-minute lock).

**If locked due to failed attempts:** Wait 30 minutes and try again, or use **Forgot Password** to reset your password.

**If locked due to suspected unauthorized access:** Contact our security team at security@shopease.com with:
- The email address on your account
- Your registered mobile number for identity verification
- The last order number you can recall

We typically restore access within 2–4 hours.

## Account Settings

### How do I add or manage delivery addresses?

To manage delivery addresses:

1. Go to **Account Settings** → **Saved Addresses**
2. Click **Add New Address** to add an address
3. Fill in: Name, phone number, flat/house number, street, area, city, state, pin code
4. Set a label (Home, Work, Other)
5. Tick **Set as Default** if you want this address pre-selected at checkout

You can save up to **10 delivery addresses**. To delete an address, click the three-dot menu next to it and select **Delete**.

---

### How does the Wishlist work?

The ShopEase Wishlist lets you save products you are interested in:

- Click the **heart icon** on any product page or search result to add it to your wishlist
- Access your wishlist via **Account** → **Wishlist** or the heart icon in the top navigation
- Share your wishlist with a unique link (great for gifting hints)
- Move items from wishlist to cart with one click
- Receive price drop alerts for wishlist items (enable in notification settings)

Wishlists are saved to your account and accessible across all devices.

---

### How do I manage my notification preferences?

Control what notifications ShopEase sends you:

1. Go to **Account Settings** → **Notifications**
2. Toggle each notification type:
   - Order updates (recommended: always on)
   - Shipping and delivery alerts (recommended: always on)
   - Price drop alerts for wishlist items
   - New arrivals in your preferred categories
   - Promotional offers and flash sales
   - ShopEase newsletter
3. Choose your preferred channels: Email, SMS, Push notification (app)

**Unsubscribing from marketing emails:** Click **Unsubscribe** at the bottom of any marketing email. This only unsubscribes you from marketing — you will still receive transactional emails (order confirmations, shipping updates).

## Account Deactivation and Deletion

### What is the difference between account deactivation and deletion?

| | Deactivation | Deletion |
|---|---|---|
| What it does | Temporarily disables your account | Permanently removes your account |
| Your data | Retained, recoverable | Deleted (except legally required records) |
| Reactivation | Sign in anytime to reactivate | Cannot be undone |
| Pending orders | Must resolve before deactivating | Must resolve before deleting |
| Loyalty points | Preserved | Lost permanently |

---

### How do I deactivate my account?

1. Go to **Account Settings** → **Security & Privacy** → **Deactivate Account**
2. Enter your password to confirm
3. Select a reason for deactivation (optional)
4. Click **Deactivate**

Your account will be inactive immediately. To reactivate, simply sign in with your email and password.

---

### How do I permanently delete my account?

1. Ensure you have no pending orders, active returns, or outstanding ShopEase Wallet balance
2. Go to **Account Settings** → **Security & Privacy** → **Delete Account**
3. Enter your password and verify with an OTP sent to your mobile number
4. Read the deletion summary (data that will be removed, data retained for legal reasons)
5. Click **Confirm Permanent Deletion**

Your account will be scheduled for deletion within 30 days. During this period you can cancel the deletion by signing in. After 30 days, deletion is permanent and irreversible.
