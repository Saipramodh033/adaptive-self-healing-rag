# ShopEase Plus Subscription
Source: faqs/subscriptions_and_shopease_plus.md

**Last Updated:** January 2025
**Applies to:** All orders on ShopEase.com and the ShopEase mobile app

## What is ShopEase Plus?
ShopEase Plus is our premium membership program designed for frequent shoppers.

- **Cost:** Rs. 499 per year.
- **Benefits:**
  - Free standard delivery (5–7 business days) on all orders, with no minimum spend threshold. (Normally, free shipping requires spending above Rs. 499).
  - 24-hour early access to major sale events.
  - Priority routing for customer support tickets.

## Cancellation and Refunds
You can cancel your ShopEase Plus membership at any time:
- **Full Refund:** If you cancel within **7 days** of signing up AND have not used any of the benefits (e.g., placing a free shipping order).
- **Prorated Refund:** If you cancel after 7 days, or have already used a benefit, you are not eligible for a refund. You will retain access to the benefits until the end of your billing cycle.

## Managing Auto-Renewal
By default, ShopEase Plus auto-renews annually. To opt-out:
1. Go to **My Account**.
2. Select **Manage Subscriptions**.
3. Toggle "Auto-Renewal" to OFF.
