# Account Security
Source: troubleshooting/account_security.md

**Last Updated:** January 2025
**Applies to:** All orders on ShopEase.com and the ShopEase mobile app

## Account Suspensions
Your account may be temporarily suspended to protect your data. Common reasons include:
- Multiple failed login attempts.
- Suspected fraudulent activity.
- Policy violations.

**Unlock process:** For failed logins, the account will automatically unlock after **30 minutes**. If your account is locked for other reasons, or you need immediate access, contact **security@shopease.com**.

## Unauthorised Access
If you suspect someone else is using your account:
1. Immediately perform a password reset via the "Forgot Password" link on the sign-in page.
2. Email our security team at **security@shopease.com**.

## Identity Verification
When contacting security support, we will ask you to verify your identity. You will need:
- Your registered account email address.
- Your registered mobile number.
- The order number of your last purchase.

## Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
We strongly recommend enabling 2FA. 
1. Go to **My Account**.
2. Select **Security Settings**.
3. Toggle "Two-Factor Authentication" to ON and follow the SMS setup instructions.
