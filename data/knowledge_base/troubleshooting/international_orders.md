# International Orders
Source: troubleshooting/international_orders.md

**Last Updated:** January 2025
**Applies to:** All orders on ShopEase.com and the ShopEase mobile app

## Supported Regions
We currently offer international shipping to the following regions:
- North America (US, Canada)
- Europe (UK, Germany, France)
- Asia-Pacific (Australia, Singapore)

We do not currently ship to regions outside of this list.

## Customs and Duties
For all international orders, the **buyer is responsible for paying any applicable customs duties, import taxes, or local clearance fees**. 
- These charges are NOT included in the checkout price.
- The local customs office will contact you for payment before the final delivery is made.

## Restricted Items
Certain items cannot be shipped internationally due to customs regulations:
- Electronics with large lithium-ion batteries.
- Liquids and pressurized aerosols.
- Perishable foods.

## International Returns
International orders are eligible for the standard **30-day return window**. However:
- ShopEase does not provide prepaid return labels for international orders.
- The **buyer must pay for return shipping** to our central warehouse.
- Original shipping fees and any customs duties paid are non-refundable.
