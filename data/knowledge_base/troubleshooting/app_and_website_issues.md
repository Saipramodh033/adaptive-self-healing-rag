# App and Website Troubleshooting
Source: troubleshooting/app_and_website_issues.md

**Last Updated:** January 2025
**Applies to:** All orders on ShopEase.com and the ShopEase mobile app

## Supported App Versions
To ensure the best experience and security, please ensure your device meets these requirements:
- **iOS:** Requires iOS 13 or later.
- **Android:** Requires Android 8 or later.
Older versions may experience degraded performance or broken checkout flows.

## General Troubleshooting Steps
If you are experiencing slow loading, items not adding to the cart, or checkout errors:
1. **Clear Cache:** Go to your browser settings or app settings and clear the cache.
2. **Update App:** Check the App Store or Google Play Store for a pending update.
3. **Re-login:** Sign out of your account and sign back in to force an account sync.

## OTP (One-Time Password) Issues
If you are not receiving the OTP for login or checkout:
1. Check your spam/junk folder if using email.
2. Wait 2 minutes for network delays.
3. Use the **"Call to get OTP"** option on the screen.

## Common Error Codes
- **Error 404:** Page not found. The product may have been removed or the link is expired.
- **Error 500:** Internal server error. We are experiencing high traffic; please try again in 5 minutes.
- **Error 1020:** Checkout blocked. This happens if our security system flags unusual network activity (e.g., using a proxy or VPN). Please disable your VPN to proceed.
