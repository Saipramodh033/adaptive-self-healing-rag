# Delivery Issues — Troubleshooting Guide

**Source:** troubleshooting/delivery_issues.md
**Last Updated:** January 2025

---

## Overview

This guide covers the most common delivery problems and the exact steps to resolve each one. Most delivery issues can be resolved within 48–72 hours.

---

## Late Delivery

### My order has not arrived by the estimated delivery date

**Step 1: Check your tracking**
- Go to **My Orders** → select the order → **Track Package**
- Review the latest status update and the timestamp

**Step 2: Understand common tracking statuses**

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Order placed | ShopEase has received your order |
| Seller confirmed | Seller has accepted and is preparing the order |
| Dispatched / Shipped | Order handed to the courier |
| In transit | Package is moving through the courier network |
| Out for delivery | Package is on the delivery vehicle today |
| Delivery attempted | Courier tried to deliver but was unsuccessful |
| Delivered | Package marked as delivered by the courier |

**Step 3: Wait 24 hours beyond the estimated date**
Delivery timelines are estimates. Factors that can cause delays:
- Public holidays or local strikes
- High order volumes during sale seasons (Diwali, New Year)
- Bad weather affecting logistics routes
- Customs delays (international orders)

**Step 4: If no movement for 48+ hours**
If your tracking shows no updates for more than 48 hours, the package may be stuck or lost. Contact ShopEase support with your order number. We will raise an investigation with the courier within 24 hours.

**Late delivery escalation steps:**
1. Report via **My Orders** → **Report a Problem** → **Late Delivery**
2. ShopEase contacts the logistics partner within 24 hours
3. If the package is located: re-delivery is arranged
4. If the package is confirmed lost: replacement or full refund within 5–7 business days

## Wrong Item Received

### I received a different product than what I ordered

If you received a completely different item from what you ordered:

1. Do NOT use or open the item if possible
2. Go to **My Orders** → find the order → **Return Item**
3. Select return reason: **Wrong item received**
4. Upload photos showing:
   - The item you received
   - The packaging label (showing your address and order number)
5. ShopEase will arrange a free pick-up of the wrong item
6. A replacement or full refund is issued within 5–7 business days

**You will NOT be charged for return shipping** in this case.

## Damaged Package

### My package arrived damaged

If your package arrived visibly damaged:

**For external damage (box crushed, wet, or torn):**
1. Do NOT sign for/accept the delivery if damage is visible at the door
2. If already accepted, take photos of the damage immediately
3. Go to **My Orders** → **Report a Problem** → **Damaged Package**
4. Upload photos of the damage to the packaging and any damaged items inside
5. Report within **48 hours of delivery**

**For items damaged inside intact packaging:**
1. Photograph the damaged item alongside the packaging
2. Report via the same process above
3. ShopEase will arrange pick-up and send a replacement or issue a full refund

**If you signed for a package without inspecting it:**
- You still have 48 hours to report damage after delivery
- Always inspect packages before signing whenever possible

## Missing Package (Marked Delivered But Not Received)

### My order is marked "Delivered" but I did not receive it

This is the most urgent type of delivery issue. Act immediately.

**Step 1: Check around your delivery address**
- Check near your door, building reception, with neighbours, or in a mailbox
- Check if a family member or colleague accepted the delivery on your behalf
- Look for a "delivery slip" or missed delivery notice

**Step 2: Verify the delivery address on your order**
Go to **My Orders** → view your order to confirm the delivery address is correct.

**Step 3: Contact ShopEase within 48 hours of the marked delivery time**
- Go to **My Orders** → **Report a Problem** → **Item Not Received**
- Or call 1-800-SHOP-EASE immediately

**What happens next:**
1. ShopEase initiates a courier investigation within 24 hours
2. The courier checks GPS delivery confirmation, proof-of-delivery photos (if taken), and driver records
3. Investigation typically takes 3–5 business days
4. If the package is confirmed not delivered: you receive a replacement or full refund
5. If the courier confirms delivery at your address: a more detailed investigation is required (may involve local authorities for high-value items)

**Important:** For orders above Rs. 5,000, report non-delivery within **24 hours** for faster resolution.

## Delivery Attempt Failed

### Courier says "Delivery Attempted" but I was home

If your tracking shows a delivery attempt but you did not receive a knock or call:

1. Check if your contact number is correctly listed on the order (visible in **My Orders** → order details)
2. Some couriers leave a notice slip — check your door and letterbox
3. Check the courier's local facility — packages are often held there for 5 business days after a failed attempt
4. Contact ShopEase support to arrange a specific re-delivery time slot

**Re-delivery options:**
- Schedule re-delivery via the courier's tracking page (link provided in your tracking notification)
- Request re-delivery to an alternative address via ShopEase support (before the package is returned)
- Self-collect from the courier's nearest facility (bring your order confirmation email and ID)
