# Website and App Errors — Troubleshooting Guide

**Source:** troubleshooting/website_errors.md
**Last Updated:** January 2025

---

## Overview

This guide covers common technical issues on the ShopEase website and mobile app, with self-service fixes for most problems.

---

## Cart and Checkout Errors

### Items disappearing from my cart

Cart items may be removed automatically when:
- The item goes out of stock while it is in your cart
- A limited-time offer associated with the item expires
- Your session times out after a long period of inactivity (browser only)
- You are not signed in and cleared your browser cookies

**Fix:**
- Sign in to your account — cart items are saved across devices when you are logged in
- If an item is out of stock, use **Notify Me** to get an alert when it is available again
- For browser sessions: sign in before adding items to ensure persistence

---

### "Item no longer available" error at checkout

This means the product sold out between the time you added it to your cart and when you attempted to checkout. Cart does not reserve stock — only completing the purchase does.

**Fix:**
- Check for alternative sellers for the same product on the product page
- Enable **Notify Me** for restocking alerts
- Look for similar products using the **Customers also bought** or **Similar items** sections

---

### Checkout page not loading or freezing

**Step 1:** Refresh the page (F5 or Ctrl+R). If the cart total and items look correct after refresh, proceed normally.

**Step 2:** Clear browser cache and cookies:
- Chrome: Settings → Privacy → Clear browsing data → Cookies and cached images → Clear
- Firefox: Settings → Privacy → Clear Data
- Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data → Remove All

**Step 3:** Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari).

**Step 4:** Disable browser extensions, especially ad blockers and VPNs, which can interfere with payment page loading.

**Step 5:** Try the ShopEase mobile app — often more reliable for checkout.

---

### Payment page shows blank/white screen

This usually happens when a browser extension or security software blocks the payment gateway's scripts.

**Fix:**
- Disable ad blockers and privacy extensions for shopease.com
- Add shopease.com and payment.shopease.com to your browser's allowed list
- Disable "Strict Mode" in Firefox Enhanced Tracking Protection for this site
- Switch to the mobile app for payment

## Login Issues

### Cannot sign in — "Incorrect email or password"

1. Verify you are using the correct email address (try your other email addresses)
2. Check that Caps Lock is off
3. Use **Forgot Password** to reset — this confirms which email is registered
4. If you previously signed in via Google or Facebook, use those buttons instead of the email/password form

---

### Account says "suspended" or "temporarily blocked"

Your account may be suspended for:
- Multiple failed login attempts (temporary 30-minute lock)
- Suspected fraudulent activity
- Policy violations flagged by our security team

**For login attempt locks:** Wait 30 minutes and try again, or use **Forgot Password** to reset.

**For security-flagged suspension:** Email security@shopease.com with your account email and registered mobile number. Our security team reviews and responds within 2–4 hours.

---

### Session keeps expiring / I am logged out frequently

ShopEase sessions expire after 30 days of inactivity on web browsers for security. On the mobile app, sessions are persistent unless you sign out manually.

**Fixes:**
- Tick **Remember me** when signing in on the web
- Use the mobile app for longer persistent sessions
- If sessions expire immediately: clear cookies and disable browser cookie-blocking settings

## Mobile App Issues

### App crashing on launch (iOS / Android)

**Step 1:** Force-close and reopen the app
- iOS: Swipe up from the home bar, swipe the app card up to close, then reopen
- Android: Tap the square button, swipe the app to close, then reopen

**Step 2:** Update the app
- iOS: App Store → Search ShopEase → Update (if available)
- Android: Play Store → ShopEase → Update

**Step 3:** Clear app cache (Android only)
- Settings → Apps → ShopEase → Storage → Clear Cache

**Step 4:** Restart your phone

**Step 5:** Uninstall and reinstall the app (your account and data are saved in the cloud)

---

### App not loading images or showing broken images

- Check your internet connection (try switching between Wi-Fi and mobile data)
- Clear the app cache (Android: Settings → Apps → ShopEase → Storage → Clear Cache)
- Check if ShopEase is having a service disruption: shopease.com/status

---

### Cannot receive OTP for app login

- Verify your registered mobile number in Account Settings
- Ensure you have network/SMS reception
- Wait 2 minutes before requesting a new OTP
- Check if your carrier is blocking short-code SMS messages (contact your carrier if so)
- Try the email OTP option if available

## Website Outages and Service Disruptions

### ShopEase is down or very slow

Check the ShopEase service status page: **shopease.com/status**

This page shows real-time status for:
- Website and mobile app
- Payment processing
- Order tracking
- Search functionality

If a service disruption is reported, it is being actively worked on. No action needed on your end — try again after the issue is resolved.

For planned maintenance, ShopEase announces downtime at least 24 hours in advance via email and the status page.
