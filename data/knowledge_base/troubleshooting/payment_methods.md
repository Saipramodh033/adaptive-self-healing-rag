# Payment Methods and Troubleshooting
Source: troubleshooting/payment_methods.md

**Last Updated:** January 2025
**Applies to:** All orders on ShopEase.com and the ShopEase mobile app

## Accepted Payment Methods
ShopEase accepts the following payment methods:
- Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, Amex, RuPay)
- UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM)
- Net Banking (All major banks)
- ShopEase Wallet
- Cash on Delivery (COD)

## Cash on Delivery (COD) Rules
COD is available on select pin codes only. 
- You can check COD availability on the product page by entering your pin code.
- The maximum order value for COD is **Rs. 10,000**. Orders above this amount must be prepaid.

## EMI Availability
Equated Monthly Installments (EMI) are available on orders above **Rs. 3,000**.
- You can choose 3, 6, or 12-month tenures at checkout using eligible credit cards.

## Payment Failure Troubleshooting
If your payment fails but money was deducted from your account:
- **Do not attempt to pay again immediately.**
- The bank will usually auto-refund the amount within 24–48 hours if the order was not generated.
- If the order is confirmed but payment status is pending, wait 15 minutes for the systems to sync.
- For unresolved deductions after 48 hours, contact your bank and our support team with the transaction ID.
