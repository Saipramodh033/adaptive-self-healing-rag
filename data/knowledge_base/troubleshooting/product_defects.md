# Product Defects — Troubleshooting Guide

**Source:** troubleshooting/product_defects.md
**Last Updated:** January 2025

---

## Overview

If you receive a product that is defective or stops working within the warranty period, this guide explains how to report it, what evidence to provide, and how to get a replacement or refund.

---

## Reporting a Product Defect

### What counts as a defect?

A defect is a problem caused by the manufacturer, not by the user. Examples include:

- Product does not turn on or function upon first use (dead on arrival / DOA)
- Missing parts or accessories inside the sealed box
- Hardware component failure under normal use
- Screen defects (dead pixels, colour bleed) that were present from the start
- Audio issues (distortion, no sound) present from unboxing
- Structural issues (cracks, misaligned parts) from manufacturing

**What is NOT a defect:**
- Damage caused by dropping the item
- Water damage (unless the product is rated waterproof)
- Wear and tear from regular use
- Performance degradation after extended use (normal for batteries, etc.)

---

## How to Report a Defect

**Within 30 days of delivery (return policy applies):**

1. Go to **My Orders** → select the product → **Return Item**
2. Select return reason: **Item arrived damaged** or **Item is defective**
3. Provide defect details (see photo evidence section below)
4. ShopEase will arrange a free pick-up
5. After inspection, you will receive a replacement or full refund within 5–7 business days

**Between 31 days and 1 year (warranty period):**

1. Go to **My Orders** → select the product → **Warranty & Service** → **Raise Warranty Claim**
2. Or email warranty@shopease.com with your order number and defect description
3. ShopEase will coordinate with the manufacturer's service network
4. Resolution via repair, replacement, or refund depending on the manufacturer's assessment

---

## Providing Photo and Video Evidence

Strong evidence speeds up your claim significantly. Provide the following:

**For DOA (Dead on Arrival) products:**
- Video showing the product not powering on despite correct charging/setup
- Photo of the product, the packaging, and the shipping label (to confirm it is your order)
- Photo of the sealed box condition at the time of opening (if taken)

**For physical defects (cracks, screen issues, etc.):**
- Close-up photo clearly showing the defect
- Photo of the full product to provide context
- Photo of the serial number on the product and/or packaging

**For functional defects (audio, connectivity, performance):**
- Video demonstrating the defect (e.g., screen flickering, speaker crackling)
- Screenshot of any error messages displayed

**Tips for effective photos/videos:**
- Ensure good lighting — blurry or dark photos delay processing
- Upload original files, not heavily compressed ones
- Multiple angles are better than one
- Include a dated newspaper or your phone's screen showing the current date in the video if possible (for time-stamping)

## Replacement vs Refund Decision

ShopEase and the manufacturer determine the resolution as follows:

| Scenario | Typical Resolution |
|---|---|
| DOA within 30 days | Replacement or full refund (customer's choice) |
| DOA within 7 days | Priority: full refund or replacement within 48 hours |
| Defect within 30 days | Replacement or full refund (customer's choice) |
| Defect between 31 days–1 year | Warranty repair, then replacement if repair fails |
| Defect after 1 year (in warranty) | Warranty repair by manufacturer service centre |
| Defect after warranty expires | Paid repair (ShopEase can connect you to service centres) |

If ShopEase offers a replacement but the exact model is out of stock:
- You may receive a replacement with equivalent or higher specifications
- Or you may opt for a full refund instead

## Manufacturer Recall Procedures

If a product you purchased is subject to a safety recall:

1. **ShopEase will contact you** via email and SMS within 24 hours of being informed of a recall
2. You are entitled to a **full refund** regardless of when you purchased the product or whether the return window has closed
3. ShopEase will arrange **free pick-up** — you do not need to pay any return shipping
4. Recalled products should **not be used** until they are returned

**Proactive check:** Visit **shopease.com/safety-recalls** to check if any of your purchased products are subject to active recalls.

**Unresponsive sellers:** If a third-party seller is unresponsive to a valid defect claim within 48 hours, contact ShopEase directly and we will intervene on your behalf.

---

## Out-of-Warranty Defects

If your product develops a defect after the warranty period:

- ShopEase can connect you to the manufacturer's authorised service centres
- Go to **shopease.com/service-centres** and enter your product brand and city
- Repair costs outside the warranty period are the customer's responsibility
- If you purchased a **ShopEase Protect** extended warranty plan, your additional coverage applies
