# Tracking Your Refund
Source: troubleshooting/refund_tracking.md

**Last Updated:** January 2025
**Applies to:** All orders on ShopEase.com and the ShopEase mobile app

## Checking Refund Status
You can track the exact status of your refund in the app:
1. Go to **My Orders**.
2. Select the returned or cancelled item.
3. Tap **Return Status**.

## Refund Status Meanings
- **Refund Initiated:** ShopEase has approved the return and sent the refund request to the payment gateway.
- **Refund Processed:** The payment gateway has confirmed the transfer to your bank.

## Processing Timelines
Refunds take time depending on the original payment method:
- **Credit/Debit Card or UPI:** 5–7 business days.
- **ShopEase Wallet:** 24–48 hours.

**Note:** Once the status shows "Refund Processed", any further delays are due to bank processing times, which are beyond ShopEase's control.

## Escalating a Stalled Refund
If it has been more than 7 business days since the refund was initiated and you still don't see it in your bank account:
- Email our dedicated returns team at **returns@shopease.com**.
- You MUST include your order number and original payment transaction ID.
