# Marketplace Seller Issues
Source: troubleshooting/seller_issues.md

**Last Updated:** January 2025
**Applies to:** All orders on ShopEase.com and the ShopEase mobile app

## How the Marketplace Works
ShopEase operates a marketplace model. While some items are "ShopEase Fulfilled" (sold and shipped by us), many items are sold by independent third-party sellers.

- **Seller Responsibility:** Third-party sellers are responsible for product quality, genuine sourcing, and answering specific product queries.
- **ShopEase Responsibility:** ShopEase guarantees payment security, enforces the 30-day standard return window, and handles the logistics for delivery and returns.

## Dispute Resolution
If you receive a counterfeit product, or an item significantly different from its description, ShopEase acts as a mediator:
1. First, initiate a standard return via **My Orders** → **Return Item**.
2. If the seller rejects the return unfairly, you can escalate the issue by emailing **support@shopease.com** with your order number and photos of the item.
3. ShopEase will investigate and, if the seller is found at fault, force the refund.

## Reporting a Seller
If you believe a seller is engaging in fraudulent behavior or selling prohibited items:
- Go to the product page.
- Scroll down to the Seller Information section.
- Click **"Report this Seller"**.

## Seller Reviews
You can leave a rating and review for a seller only after you have purchased from them. Seller ratings directly affect their visibility on the platform.
