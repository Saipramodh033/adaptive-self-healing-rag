# Electronics Policy
Source: policies/electronics_policy.md

**Last Updated:** January 2025
**Applies to:** All orders on ShopEase.com and the ShopEase mobile app

## Return Eligibility
Electronics, including mobile phones, laptops, and smart home devices, are subject to the standard **30-day return window** from delivery.

However, electronics must meet strict condition requirements:
- Items must be **unopened** in their original sealed packaging if returning due to buyer's remorse.
- If the item is **defective or dead-on-arrival (DOA)**, it may be returned opened, but you must report the defect within **48 hours of delivery**.

## Warranty Claims
- **Brand Warranty:** Most electronics carry a manufacturer's warranty. For faults developing after the 30-day return window, you must contact the brand's authorized service center directly. ShopEase is not responsible for servicing under brand warranty.
- **ShopEase Warranty:** Only applies to "ShopEase Basics" branded items.

**Important:** All electronics returns and warranty claims require the item's **IMEI or Serial Number** to be verified. The number on the item must match the number on the invoice exactly.

## Refunds
Standard refund timelines apply to eligible returns:
- Credit/Debit Card or UPI: 5–7 business days.
- ShopEase Wallet: 24–48 hours.
