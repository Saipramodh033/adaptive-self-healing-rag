# Warranty Policy — ShopEase

**Source:** policies/warranty_policy.md
**Last Updated:** January 2025
**Applies to:** Electronics, appliances, and select product categories sold on ShopEase

---

## Overview

ShopEase facilitates warranty claims on behalf of product manufacturers. Most electronics and appliances sold on ShopEase come with a **1-year manufacturer warranty** unless otherwise stated on the product listing.

---

## Warranty Coverage Period

| Product Category | Warranty Period |
|---|---|
| Smartphones and tablets | 1 year (manufacturer) |
| Laptops and computers | 1 year (manufacturer) |
| Home appliances (washing machines, refrigerators) | 1–5 years (varies by brand) |
| Small kitchen appliances | 1 year (manufacturer) |
| Audio and headphones | 6 months – 1 year (varies) |
| Smartwatches and fitness bands | 1 year (manufacturer) |
| Cameras and accessories | 1 year (manufacturer) |
| Power banks and chargers | 6 months (manufacturer) |
| Clothing, footwear, accessories | No warranty (covered by return policy) |

**Extended warranties** are offered by ShopEase for an additional fee at checkout for select categories (1, 2, or 3 year plans).

---

## What the Warranty Covers

The standard manufacturer warranty covers:

- Manufacturing defects (faulty components, assembly errors)
- Hardware failures under normal usage conditions
- Malfunctions that occur without physical damage by the user
- Dead-on-arrival (DOA) products

---

## What the Warranty Does NOT Cover

The warranty is void and does not cover:

- Physical damage caused by the user (drops, water damage, broken screens)
- Damage from misuse, unauthorized repair, or modification
- Wear and tear from normal usage (scratches, fading)
- Damage from power surges, flooding, or other external causes
- Products with removed, altered, or tampered serial numbers
- Software issues, viruses, or data loss
- Consumable parts (batteries, bulbs) after normal usage

## How to Make a Warranty Claim

**Step 1: Check your warranty status**
Go to **My Orders** → select the product → **Warranty & Service** to verify your warranty validity and expiry date.

**Step 2: Contact support**
- Use the **Raise Warranty Claim** button in your order details
- Or email warranty@shopease.com with your order number, product serial number, and a description of the defect

**Step 3: Provide documentation**
You may be asked to provide:
- Photos or videos demonstrating the defect
- Proof of purchase (order number)
- Product serial number (found on the device or its packaging)

**Step 4: Device inspection**
Depending on the product and brand:
- **In-home service:** A technician visits your home (available for large appliances in major cities)
- **Drop-off at service centre:** ShopEase will direct you to the nearest authorized service centre for the brand
- **Pick-up and repair:** ShopEase arranges courier pick-up, repair, and re-delivery

**Step 5: Resolution**
After inspection, the resolution may be:
- **Repair:** Defective components replaced, covered by warranty
- **Replacement:** New unit provided if the defect cannot be repaired
- **Refund:** Issued if repair/replacement is not feasible within a reasonable timeframe

## Manufacturer Recall Procedures

If a product you purchased from ShopEase is subject to a manufacturer safety recall:

1. ShopEase will contact you via email and SMS within 24 hours of being notified
2. You are entitled to a full refund, regardless of return window
3. ShopEase will arrange free pick-up of the recalled product
4. No penalty or deduction applies to recall-related returns

Stay informed about recalls: Visit **shopease.com/safety-recalls** for current notices.

## ShopEase Extended Warranty (ShopEase Protect)

ShopEase Protect extends coverage beyond the manufacturer warranty:

| Plan | Duration | Coverage |
|---|---|---|
| Basic | 1 year | Manufacturing defects (same as manufacturer warranty) |
| Plus | 2 years | Manufacturing defects + accidental damage (1 incident) |
| Premium | 3 years | Manufacturing defects + accidental damage (2 incidents) + theft |

Extended warranty plans can be purchased at checkout or within 30 days of delivery from **My Orders** → **Add Extended Warranty**.
