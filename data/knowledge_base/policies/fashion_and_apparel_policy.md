# Fashion and Apparel Policy
Source: policies/fashion_and_apparel_policy.md

**Last Updated:** January 2025
**Applies to:** All orders on ShopEase.com and the ShopEase mobile app

## Return Window and Conditions
Apparel, clothing, and fashion accessories fall under our standard **30-day return window** from the date of delivery.

To be eligible for a return:
- Items must be **unworn, unwashed, and undamaged**.
- All original tags must be attached.
- Footwear must be returned in the original, undamaged shoe box. (Please do not use the shoe box as the shipping box).

## Non-Returnable Items
For hygiene reasons, the following items are strictly **NOT eligible for return**:
- Swimwear
- Innerwear and lingerie
- Socks
- Earrings

## Exchanges
ShopEase does not offer a direct exchange system. If you need a different size or color:
1. Initiate a return for the unwanted item via **My Orders** → **Return Item**.
2. Place a new order for the correct size or color.
Your refund for the returned item will be processed normally (5–7 business days to card/UPI, or 24–48 hours to Wallet).
