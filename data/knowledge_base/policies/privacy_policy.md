## Overview

ShopEase ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and what rights you have over your information.

By using ShopEase.com or our mobile app, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

---

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide Directly

- **Account information:** Name, email address, phone number, password (stored encrypted)
- **Delivery information:** Shipping addresses, delivery instructions
- **Payment information:** We do NOT store full card numbers. Payments are processed by PCI-DSS compliant third-party gateways (Razorpay, PayPal, Stripe)
- **Communications:** Messages sent to customer support, product reviews, ratings

### Information Collected Automatically

- **Device information:** Browser type, operating system, device model, IP address
- **Usage data:** Pages visited, products viewed, search queries, time spent on site
- **Location data:** Approximate location derived from IP address; precise location (with your permission) for same-day delivery
- **Cookies and tracking:** See the Cookies section below

### Information from Third Parties

- **Social login:** If you sign in via Google or Facebook, we receive your name, email, and profile picture from that platform
- **Payment gateways:** Transaction confirmation and fraud signals (not card details)
- **Delivery partners:** Tracking updates, delivery confirmation

## Cookies Policy

ShopEase uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience.

**Types of cookies we use:**

- **Essential cookies:** Required for the website to function (login sessions, cart contents). Cannot be disabled.
- **Analytics cookies:** Help us understand how users interact with our site (Google Analytics). Can be disabled.
- **Marketing cookies:** Used to show you relevant ads on other platforms (Google Ads, Meta). Can be disabled.
- **Preference cookies:** Remember your language, currency, and display preferences. Can be disabled.

**Managing cookies:** You can manage cookie preferences in your account settings under **Privacy & Cookies**, or via your browser settings. Disabling non-essential cookies will not affect your ability to shop.

## Third-Party Data Sharing

We share your data with the following categories of third parties:

- **Logistics partners** (Delhivery, Blue Dart, FedEx): Name, address, phone number for delivery
- **Payment processors** (Razorpay, PayPal, Stripe): Transaction data for payment processing
- **Analytics providers** (Google Analytics): Anonymised usage data
- **Customer support tools** (Zendesk): Support ticket content and account info
- **Email service providers** (SendGrid): For transactional emails

We do **not** sell your personal data to advertisers or data brokers.

We may disclose your information to law enforcement or government agencies if required by law.

## Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

- **Right to access:** Request a copy of the data we hold about you
- **Right to rectification:** Ask us to correct inaccurate information
- **Right to deletion:** Request deletion of your account and associated data (subject to legal retention requirements)
- **Right to data portability:** Receive your data in a machine-readable format
- **Right to withdraw consent:** Opt out of marketing emails at any time via the unsubscribe link
- **Right to object:** Object to processing based on legitimate interest

**To exercise your rights:** Email privacy@shopease.com or use the **Privacy Centre** in your account settings. We will respond within 30 days.

---

## Account Deletion

To permanently delete your ShopEase account:

1. Go to **Account Settings** → **Security & Privacy** → **Delete Account**
2. Confirm your identity via OTP
3. Your account will be scheduled for deletion within 30 days
4. Order history is retained for 7 years for tax compliance but will not be linked to your profile

Note: Pending orders must be completed or cancelled before account deletion.

## Data Security

- All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
- Passwords are hashed using bcrypt (never stored in plain text)
- We conduct regular security audits and penetration testing
- Access to your data is restricted to authorised personnel on a need-to-know basis
