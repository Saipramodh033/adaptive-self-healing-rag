# Shipping Policy — ShopEase

**Source:** policies/shipping_policy.md
**Last Updated:** January 2025
**Applies to:** All orders placed on ShopEase.com and the ShopEase mobile app

---

## Overview

ShopEase ships across India and to selected international destinations. We partner with leading logistics providers to ensure your orders arrive safely and on time.

---

## Domestic Shipping Options (India)

| Shipping Type | Estimated Delivery | Cost |
|---|---|---|
| Standard Delivery | 5–7 business days | Rs. 49 (free above Rs. 499) |
| Express Delivery | 2–3 business days | Rs. 149 |
| Same-Day Delivery | Same day (order before 11am) | Rs. 299 |
| Next-Day Delivery | By 10pm next calendar day | Rs. 199 |

**Same-Day Delivery** is available in the following cities: Mumbai, Delhi, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Chennai, Pune, Kolkata, Ahmedabad, Jaipur, and Surat.

---

## Free Shipping Thresholds

- **Standard Delivery:** Free on all orders above Rs. 499
- **Express Delivery:** Free on all orders above Rs. 2,999
- **ShopEase Plus members:** Free Standard Delivery on all orders, regardless of order value

Free shipping is calculated on the cart total after applying discount codes but before taxes.

---

## Order Processing Time

- Orders placed before **2:00 PM IST** on a business day are processed the same day
- Orders placed after 2:00 PM or on weekends/public holidays are processed the next business day
- Custom or personalised items may require 2–5 additional business days for preparation

---

## Tracking Your Order

Once your order is shipped, you will receive:

1. A shipping confirmation email with your **tracking number**
2. An SMS to your registered mobile number
3. Real-time tracking available in **My Orders** on the app and website

Tracking updates may take **4–6 hours** to appear after the shipment is picked up. If tracking shows no movement for more than 48 hours, contact our support team.

## Customs, Duties, and Taxes (International Orders)

- ShopEase declares the accurate value of all international shipments
- **Import duties and taxes** in the destination country are the **buyer's responsibility**
- ShopEase is not responsible for delays caused by customs inspections
- Some products (electronics, restricted goods) may not be eligible for international shipping due to destination country regulations

---

## Delivery Attempt Policy

Our logistics partners will attempt delivery **3 times** before returning the package:

- **Attempt 1:** As scheduled
- **Attempt 2:** Next business day
- **Attempt 3:** Two business days after Attempt 2

If all 3 attempts fail:
- The package will be held at the nearest facility for 5 business days
- You will receive a notification to schedule re-delivery or self-pickup
- After 5 days, the package will be returned to ShopEase and a refund will be issued (minus Rs. 149 return logistics fee)

---

## Shipping Restrictions

The following items cannot be shipped to certain locations:

- **Flammable goods:** Not eligible for air shipping
- **Lithium batteries:** Restricted to ground transport only in some regions
- **Oversized items:** (above 20kg or 150cm in any dimension) — contact support for special arrangements
- **Cash on Delivery (COD):** Available only in select pin codes within India; not available for international orders

---

## Damaged or Lost Shipments

If your order arrives damaged or is lost in transit:

1. Report the issue within **48 hours of the expected delivery date**
2. Go to **My Orders** → **Report a Problem** or email support@shopease.com
3. Attach photos of damaged packaging and items
4. ShopEase will initiate an investigation with the logistics partner within 24 hours
5. Resolution (replacement or refund) is typically provided within 5–7 business days
