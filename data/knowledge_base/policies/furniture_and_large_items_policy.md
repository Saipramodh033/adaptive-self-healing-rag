# Furniture and Large Items Policy
Source: policies/furniture_and_large_items_policy.md

**Last Updated:** January 2025
**Applies to:** All orders on ShopEase.com and the ShopEase mobile app

## Special Return Window
Unlike standard items, **furniture and large appliances have a strict 14-day return window** from the date of delivery.

## Custom and Personalized Furniture
Custom-made, made-to-order, or personalized furniture items are strictly **non-returnable and non-refundable** unless they arrive damaged or defective.

## Delivery and Assembly
We offer a White-Glove Delivery Service for large items:
- The delivery team will place the item in your room of choice and perform basic assembly.
- Any transit damage must be reported within **48 hours of delivery**.
- **Returns Pickup:** If you are returning a large item, our logistics team will handle the disassembly and pickup at no extra cost. An inspection for damage will be conducted at the time of pickup.

## Refunds
Refunds are processed after the item passes the pickup inspection and reaches our warehouse.
- Credit/Debit Card or UPI: 5–7 business days.
- ShopEase Wallet: 24–48 hours.
