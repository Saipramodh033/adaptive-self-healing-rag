# Refund and Returns Policy — ShopEase

**Source:** policies/refund_and_returns.md
**Last Updated:** January 2025
**Applies to:** All orders placed on ShopEase.com and the ShopEase mobile app

---

## Overview

ShopEase offers a straightforward 30-day return policy on most items. We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not happy for any reason, we will work with you to resolve the issue.

---

## Return Window

- **Standard items:** 30 days from the date of delivery
- **Electronics and appliances:** 30 days from the date of delivery (must be unopened or defective)
- **Clothing and footwear:** 30 days from the date of delivery (unworn, tags attached)
- **Furniture and large items:** 14 days from the date of delivery (due to logistics costs)
- **Perishable goods:** Not eligible for return unless delivered damaged or incorrect

The return window starts on the day your order is marked as delivered in our system — not the day you placed the order.

---

## Item Condition Requirements

To be eligible for a return, items must meet the following conditions:

- **Original condition:** Unused, unwashed, and undamaged
- **Original packaging:** Items must be returned in their original box or packaging
- **Tags and accessories:** All original tags, manuals, cables, and accessories must be included
- **Proof of purchase:** Your ShopEase order number must be referenced in the return request

If an item is returned in a condition that does not meet these requirements, ShopEase reserves the right to issue a partial refund or deny the return.

---

## Items NOT Eligible for Return

The following categories cannot be returned:

- **Digital products:** Software licenses, e-books, downloadable content, gift cards
- **Perishable goods:** Food, beverages, flowers, and plants
- **Personal hygiene items:** Opened cosmetics, earrings, underwear, swimwear
- **Customized items:** Products personalized with names, engravings, or custom designs
- **Sale items marked Final Sale:** Clearly labeled at time of purchase
- **Hazardous materials:** Batteries, chemicals, and flammable goods

---

## How to Initiate a Return

**Step 1:** Log in to your ShopEase account at shopease.com

**Step 2:** Go to **My Orders** and locate the order you want to return

**Step 3:** Click **Return Item** next to the product

**Step 4:** Select a return reason from the dropdown menu:
  - Changed my mind
  - Wrong item received
  - Item arrived damaged
  - Item does not match description
  - Other

**Step 5:** Choose your preferred return method:
  - **Drop-off at ShopEase Return Centre** (free)
  - **Courier pick-up from your address** (free for defective/wrong items; Rs. 99 for change-of-mind returns)

**Step 6:** Print the prepaid return label provided (or show QR code at drop-off point)

**Step 7:** Pack the item securely and drop it off or wait for the courier

---

## Refund Processing Timeline

Once we receive and inspect your return:

| Refund Method | Processing Time |
|---|---|
| Original payment method (card/UPI) | 5–7 business days |
| ShopEase Wallet | 24–48 hours |
| Bank transfer (NEFT) | 7–10 business days |

You will receive an email confirmation when your refund has been initiated. The time it takes to appear in your account depends on your bank's processing speed.

---

## Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may be issued in the following situations:

- Item returned without original packaging (deduction of up to 20%)
- Item shows signs of use beyond inspection (deduction based on condition)
- Missing accessories or manuals (deduction based on accessory value)
- Late returns accepted as exceptions (subject to management discretion)

---

## Exchange Policy

ShopEase does not offer direct exchanges. To get a different size, colour, or model:

1. Initiate a return for the original item
2. Place a new order for the item you want

If you are exchanging due to a defect, your return shipping will be free and your refund will be prioritized.

---

## Defective or Wrong Items

If you received a defective or incorrect item, we cover all return costs and expedite your refund or replacement. Contact us within **48 hours of delivery** by:

- Using the **Report a Problem** button in My Orders
- Emailing support@shopease.com with photos of the issue
- Calling 1-800-SHOP-EASE
