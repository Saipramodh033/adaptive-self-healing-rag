# Perishables and Grocery Policy
Source: policies/perishables_and_grocery_policy.md

**Last Updated:** January 2025
**Applies to:** All orders on ShopEase.com and the ShopEase mobile app

## Non-Returnable Rule
Due to food safety regulations and the nature of the products, all **perishables, fresh produce, and grocery items are strictly NON-RETURNABLE**.

## Exceptions (Wrong or Damaged Delivery)
We will issue a refund or replacement only in the following scenarios:
- The item delivered was damaged or spoiled.
- The item delivered was incorrect or missing from your order.
- The item delivered was past its printed expiry date.

### Crucial Reporting Window
For grocery and perishables, you MUST report any issues within **2 hours of delivery**. 
Go to **My Orders** → **Report a Problem**. Claims made after 2 hours will not be honored.

### Item-Level Refunds
If a grocery order has missing or spoiled items, we process an **item-level refund** for only those specific items, rather than cancelling the entire order. 
Refunds follow standard timelines:
- Credit/Debit Card or UPI: 5–7 business days.
- ShopEase Wallet: 24–48 hours.
