# Bulk and Business Orders
Source: policies/bulk_and_business_orders.md

**Last Updated:** January 2025
**Applies to:** All orders on ShopEase.com and the ShopEase mobile app

## Business Pricing
The standard ShopEase storefront does NOT show bulk prices. If you require bulk quantities, you must contact our business team directly.

To qualify for bulk pricing, your order must meet a minimum of **10 units per SKU**. 

## Requesting a Quote
To request a bulk order quote:
1. Email our dedicated business team at **business@shopease.com**.
2. Include the SKU, quantity needed, and your delivery pin code.
3. Our team typically provides a quote turnaround in **2–3 business days**.

## Business Accounts
For frequent bulk buyers, we offer dedicated Business Accounts which include:
- A dedicated account manager.
- Custom pricing agreements.
- Invoicing tailored for B2B transactions.

Contact **business@shopease.com** to set up a business account.

## Support for Existing Orders
For general support, you may use our standard channels, but for issues relating specifically to business terms or bulk pricing:
- Email: business@shopease.com
- Phone: 1-800-SHOP-EASE (Monday–Friday, 9 AM–6 PM IST)
- Live Chat: shopease.com (Monday–Saturday, 9 AM–8 PM IST)
