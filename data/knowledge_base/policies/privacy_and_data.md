# Privacy and Data
Source: policies/privacy_and_data.md

**Last Updated:** January 2025
**Applies to:** All users on ShopEase.com and the ShopEase mobile app

## Data Collection
ShopEase collects personal data required to fulfill your orders and improve our services. This includes:
- Name, delivery address, and contact details.
- Order history and browsing behavior.
- Payment transaction IDs (Note: We do not store full credit card numbers; these are handled securely by our payment gateways).

## Data Sharing and Sales
**ShopEase does not sell your personal data to third parties.** Data is only shared with trusted logistics and payment partners necessary to fulfill your order.

## Deleting Your Account and Data
Under the DPDP Act, you have the right to request the deletion of your personal data.
To delete your account:
1. Go to **My Account** → **Privacy Settings**.
2. Click **Delete Account**.
3. This process takes up to 30 days. Note that we are required by law to retain basic transaction records for tax and auditing purposes.
