# Order Cancellation Policy
Source: policies/cancellation_policy.md

**Last Updated:** January 2025
**Applies to:** All orders on ShopEase.com and the ShopEase mobile app

## When Can I Cancel?
- **Before Shipment:** Orders can be freely cancelled before they have shipped. Go to **My Orders** → Select the order → Click **Cancel Order**.
- **In Transit:** Orders that have already shipped cannot be cancelled. You must wait for delivery and then initiate a return (within the 30-day standard window, or 14-day window for furniture).
- **Same-day/Express Orders:** Due to fast processing, the cancellation window is strictly **30 minutes** from order placement.

## Failed Deliveries
If the courier misses the delivery or you reject the package at the door, ShopEase cannot manually cancel the order on your behalf while it is in the courier network. 
You must wait for the package to be processed as "Return-to-Origin" (RTO). Once the RTO is complete, your refund will be processed. We allow a maximum of **3 delivery attempts**.

## Cancellation Refunds
If you cancel an order before shipment, the refund timeline is the same as a standard return:
- **Credit/Debit Card or UPI:** 5–7 business days.
- **ShopEase Wallet:** 24–48 hours.
