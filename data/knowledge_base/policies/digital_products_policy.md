# Digital Products and Downloads Policy
Source: policies/digital_products_policy.md

**Last Updated:** January 2025
**Applies to:** All orders on ShopEase.com and the ShopEase mobile app

## Return and Refund Eligibility
Due to their nature, **digital products (e-books, software licenses, game keys, downloadable content)** are generally **NOT eligible for return or refund**. Once a digital code is generated or a file is downloaded, the sale is final.

### Exceptions for Corrupted Files
The only exception to the non-refundable rule is if the digital file is corrupted or undownloadable. 
If you experience this issue:
1. You must report it within **48 hours of delivery**.
2. Go to **My Orders** → Select the item → Click **Report a Problem** → Choose "Digital product issue".
3. Our team will verify the corruption and, if confirmed, process a full refund.

## Gift Cards and Subscriptions
- **Gift Cards:** Digital and physical gift cards are non-refundable once issued.
- **Streaming Access Codes:** These are non-transferable and non-refundable.

## Refund Timelines
If an exception is granted for a corrupted file:
- **Credit/Debit Card or UPI:** 5–7 business days.
- **ShopEase Wallet:** 24–48 hours.
